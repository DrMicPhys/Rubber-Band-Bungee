Why Rubber Band Bungee Jump Activities

Elastic cords provide an excellent framework to explore forces, work and energy.  A Bungee Jump activity (with inanimate "jumpers") are a dynamic manifistation of these concepts.  In tis activity students will design, build and test a bungee cord to safely drop the jumper at a designated drop site.
